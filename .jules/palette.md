## 2024-04-30 - Missing Label Associations in Auth Forms | Learning: Screen reader users and those with cognitive disabilities rely on programmatically associated labels for form inputs. The auth forms lacked explicit 'for'/'id' linkages. | Action: Added explicit 'for' and 'id' attributes to all form inputs in login and register pages, and added an 'aria-label' to the password visibility toggle in login.vue.
