## 2024-05-23 - Replaced window.alert with Sonner Toast | Learning: Native `alert()` blocks the main thread and creates poor UX. The app relied heavily on `alert()` for form error feedback, which broke the immersive experience of the Nuxt/shadcn-vue stack. | Action: Added shadcn's `sonner` component and `vue-sonner` integration in `app.vue`. Replaced `alert()` calls in `create` and `edit` pages with the non-blocking, stylized `toast` utility from `vue-sonner`.
